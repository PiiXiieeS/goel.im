About me.

Originally from New Delhi, India, now in Seattle, WA, I'm an undergraduate at the University of Washington, working towards a BS degree in Computer Science. That means I code. My resume is available in PDF format or on LinkedIn.

I've worked with Jeff Huang where we investigated how spammers harvest email addresses and with Lydia Chilton on her Computational Humor project. In Summer 2013, I interned at Lenskart as a Data Analyst. In Summer 2014, I will intern at Google with the Street View (Maps) team in the Engineering Practicum program.

Broadly, I am interested in making apps and scripts that help improve end-user productivity, and user-facing web apps. I'm also interested in web scraping and building API's. I spend most of my (free) time on Reddit, Quora and StackOverflow.
